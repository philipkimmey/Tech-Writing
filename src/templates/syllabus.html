Course Prerequisites

  • Writing I or Equivalent
  • Junior or Senior level status

Course Description

Communication techniques taught throughout the course, along with accompanying assignments, help students improve both written language and presentation skills, particularly when approaching scientific and engineering topics.

Course Objectives

  • Students will be able to present ideas about complex technical subjects in a succinct, organized way to an appropriate audience
  • Students will learn both general and specialized principles of effective technical communication
  • Students will use the knowledge and skills they gain in this course to write and present confidently and successfully.
